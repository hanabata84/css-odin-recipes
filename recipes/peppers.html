Air Fryer Stuffed Mini Peppers

These quick air fried stuffed mini pepper appetizers are ready in less than 15 minutes and require just three ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Peppers
  • Prosciutto
  • Cheese Spread
  • Garlic
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut each pepper in half lengthwise, and remove seeds and stem.
  3. Slice prosciutto into pieces the size of pepper halves, and lay one piece in each pepper half.
  4. Spread 1 tablespoon cheese into each pepper half.
  5. If prosciutto hangs over pepper sides, fold it upward to cover cheese.
  6. Air fry until cheese is very warm and soft, about 5 minutes.
